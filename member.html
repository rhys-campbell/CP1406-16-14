Make A Donation

With a philanthropic gift of $200 or more (we'll leave that up to you), you can become an Orchestra Benefactor and be placed on our Benefactor roll for twelve months. This roll will be published in each concert program and on our website for twelve months. As a Benefactor you will also receive two free tickets to our next concert, be placed on the mailing list for our newsletter, and will be a guest at our annual Season's Launch function. Please complete the details below and mail to PO Box 576 Townsville 4810, make cheques payable to North Queensland Ensembles Inc. or you can fill out the below form, and pay by direct debit to BSB 034 222, Account No. 173922. Donations are tax deductible. For more information contact Simon McConnell on 0418776961 or email mcconnellgray@optusnet.com.au. Your support of the Orchestra will be greatly appreciated.
