Goals

Concisely state what the goals (purposes) of the website are. Note that the goals must be to improve something in a measurable way. Why is the site needed? How does it benefit the client? Be clear and specific. Good goal statements are only a few sentences and contain no unnecessary information.

With focus on local community and love for the musical arts, the website is to entice the customer to purchase tickets for the next performance. Complementary to this is to influence the customer to sign up for membership and engage in sponsorship of the orchestra. Completion of these goals will result in an increase in revenue for the Barrier Reef Orchestra.
