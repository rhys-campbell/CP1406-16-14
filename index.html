Saturday, May 7th - We have our Annual General Meeting at 2pm at the North Queensland Opera and Music Theatre Hall in Gill Park, off Hugh Street. The meeting will be followed by refreshments and a Forum giving people the opportunity to put forward Ideas about the orchestra.

Sunday, July 17th at 5pm - Twilight Classics with Conductor Mario Dobering. The concert will feature the winner of the 2015 ACVC (Australian Concerto and Vocal Competition). The winner, Arthur Athan from Sydeny, will play the Rachmaninov Piano Concerto No 2. The concert will also include Brahms Symphony No 4 and Queens Gardens written by Dr David Salisbury fro James Cook University.

Sunday, July 31st - The Barrier Reef Orchestra will be part of the free concert in Queens' Gardens run by the Council and the Australian Festival of Chamber Music. More

Saturday, October 22nd - The Barrier Reef Orchestra Goes to the Opera concert will begin at 7:30pm. The concert will have Craig Johnson as Conductor and will feature singer Paul Tabone who grew up in Innisfail and now sings in Italy. This concert will be repeated in Ingham Town Hall on Sunday, 29th at 3pm.

Arthur Athan

Australian Concerto and Vocal Competition Winner 2015
