Planned events

Annual General Meeting

!image here!

We have our Annual General Meeting on Saturday, 7th May at 2pm at the North Queensland Opera and Music Theatre Hall in Gill Park, off Hugh Street. The meeting will be followed by refreshments and a Forum giving people the opportunity to put forward ideas about the orchestra, possible changes etc.

Twilight Classics

!image here!

Sunday, July 17th at 5 pm we have Twilight Classics with Conductor Mario Dobering. The concert will feature the winner of the 2015 ACVC (Australian Concerto and Vocal Competition) Competition. The winner Arthur Athan from Sydney will play the Rachmaninov Piano Concerto No 2. It will also include Brahms Symphony No 4 and Queens Gardens written by Dr David Salisbury from James Cook Uni.

Queens Gardens Concert - The 1812 again!

!image here!

Free concert in celebration of the 150th Anniversary of the municipality of Townsville, co-presented with the Townsville City Council and hosted by Steve ‘Pricey’ Price. Program to include the following plus more from the 1RAR Band: VIVALDI Spring from The Four Seasons JOHN WILLIAMS Theme from Schindler’s List Tasmin Little (violin), Barrier Reef Orchestra DELIBES Les filles de Cadix VERDI Sempre Libera from La Traviata Valda Wilson (soprano), Barrier Reef Orchestra TCHAIKOVSKY 1812 Overture, Op.49 Barrier Reef Orchestra, 1RAR Band Audience participation instead of cannon fire!

Two weeks later the orchestra will be part of the free concert in Queens’s Gardens run by the Council and the Australian Festival of Chamber Music. http://www.afcm.com.au/festival/festival-program/?date=2016-07-31&id=109088&t=0

Barrier Reef Orchestra Goes to the Opera

!image here!

On Saturday, October, 22nd at 7.30pm the concert is Barrier Reef Orchestra Goes to the Opera. The concert will have Craig Johnson as Conductor and will feature singer, Paul Tabone who grew up in Innisfail and now sings in Italy. This concert will be repeated in the Ingham Town Hall on Sundaay, 29th at 3pm
